Product Release

June 5, 2021 · 2 min read

ATOP CLOUD is a technology-driven startup to streamline and democratize digital transformation in financial service sector. We are working on cloud native, Open API, and blockchain technology development to achieve this, and we provide ATOP open finance platform (OFP) to help the enterprises to get easily started with finance digital transformation journey forward financial inclusion. Thanks to easy-to-adopt ATOP OFP, any enterprise can develop and operated its differentiated financial services in a simple and hassle-free way.

ATOP OFP is developed on ATOP cloud native platform, KUPBOARD and it can support PSD2 Open Banking, GSMA Mobile Money, and Blockchain services seamlessly, which are considered open standard and fundamental financial services forward open finance movement. Kupboard offers easy and configurable method to initialize and setup the cloud native platform on any public cloud and even on bare-metal servers, so any enterprise can develop and operate its services in IaaS agnostic and Kubernetes native environment with Kupboard.

For Blockchain-based services, we have also released ATOP BLOCKCHAIN publicly. And now you can download ATOP Mining Wallet at Google Play Store and its iOS version will be available soon. ATOP Blockchain is designed to work as the technological institution for trust, transparency and traceability in financial service sector. We aim to utilize it to meet this goal. For instance, it can be used for blockchain-based real-time reconciliation and settlement in payment and remittance among financial institutions, enterprises and end customers by offering trust, transparency and traceability in cross-border and local transactions. It can play the technological institution role in reconciliation and dispute resolution. There are more use cases and we have the plan to launch astonishing blockchain-based services to make everyone’s financial life easier and better.

Mainnet Release

April 21, 2021 · One min read

스마트폰을 통한 크립토마이닝 지원을 시작으로, 오픈 파이낸스 서비스를 지향하는 ATOP 블록체인 메인넷이 출시되었습니다.

글로벌 금융 서비스의 생태계는 오픈 파이낸스로 급격하게 변화하고 있습니다. 대한민국에서도 역시 오픈뱅킹 서비스를 시작으로, 마이데이타 서비스와 마이페이먼트 서비스가 예정되어 있습니다.

이렇게 급변하는 오픈형, 즉 개방형 금융 서비스에 대응하기 위하여, 블록체인 메인넷인 ATOP 블록체인을 출시하였습니다.

ATOP Wallet 모바일 앱을 설치함으로써 ATOP 코인의 마이닝을 시작할 수 있습니다. 스마트폰이 블록체인의 노드가 되는 세상에서, ATOP 메인넷은 오픈 파이낸스 서비스를 위한 가장 안전한 블록체인이 될 것입니다.
